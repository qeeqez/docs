# Privacy Policy



**Last Updated: September 2025**



## Overview



This Privacy Policy explains how RIXL Inc. ("RIXL," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use rixl.com and our media optimization platform. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and ensuring transparent data practices.



By using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.



## Information We Collect



### Information You Provide



We collect information you provide directly to us, including:



- **Account Information**: Name, email address, company name, and billing information when you create an account

- **Profile Information**: Optional profile details, preferences, and settings

- **Content**: Media files (videos, images) you upload to our platform for processing

- **Communications**: Messages you send to us through support channels, feedback forms, or other communications

- **Payment Information**: Billing details and payment method information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)



### Information We Collect Automatically



When you use our Services, we automatically collect:



- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use our Services, including features accessed, processing requests, and performance metrics

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, and unique device identifiers

- **Log Data**: Server logs, error reports, and system activity information

- **Analytics Data**: Aggregated usage statistics and performance metrics



### Cookies and Tracking Technologies



We use cookies and similar technologies to:



- Maintain your login session and preferences

- Analyze usage patterns and improve our Services

- Provide personalized experiences

- Ensure security and prevent fraud



You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## Data Security



We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:



- Encryption of data in transit and at rest

- Regular security audits and monitoring

- Access controls and authentication

- Secure data centers and infrastructure

- Employee training on data protection



We use robust security measures, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. If a major security incident occurs, we will notify you promptly. We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

- Provide our Services to you

- Comply with legal obligations

- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

- Maintain security and prevent fraud



If you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, unless we are legally required to retain it.
