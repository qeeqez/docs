Start building professional media experiences in minutes with our intuitive platform and developer-friendly SDKs.

## How RIXL Works

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Upload Your Media" icon="upload">
    Drop files into the dashboard or upload programmatically via API. RIXL
    accepts all common video and image formats.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic Processing" icon="zap">
