## What Are Cookies

<Callout type="info">
  Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website or use our services. They improve your
  experience by saving preferences, maintaining login sessions, and providing insights into platform usage.
</Callout>

## Types of Cookies We Use

<Tabs items={['Session Cookies', 'Persistent Cookies', 'First-Party Cookies', 'Third-Party Cookies']}>
  <Tab value="Session Cookies">
    **Session Cookies**

    Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser and help maintain your session during use.

    **Examples include:**
    - Login session management
    - Shopping cart contents
    - Form data preservation
    - Security tokens

    <Callout type="info">
      These are temporary and automatically removed when you close your browser.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>

  <Tab value="Persistent Cookies">
    **Persistent Cookies**

    Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They remember your preferences
    across visits.

    **Examples include:**
    - User preferences and settings
    - Language and region choices
    - Dashboard customizations
    - "Remember me" functionality

    <Callout type="info">
      These help us remember your settings between visits.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>

  <Tab value="First-Party Cookies">
    **First-Party Cookies**

    These cookies are set directly by RIXL to provide core services and functionality.

    **We use first-party cookies for:**
    - User authentication and account management
    - Platform functionality and features
    - User interface preferences
    - Security and fraud prevention
    - Performance optimization

    <Callout type="info">
      First-party cookies are essential for our platform to function properly.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>

  <Tab value="Third-Party Cookies">
    **Third-Party Cookies**

    Third-party cookies are set by our partners and service providers to enhance our services.

    **Our partners include:**
    - Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)
    - Payment processors
    - Customer support tools
    - Content delivery networks
    - Marketing and advertising platforms

    <Callout type="warn">
      You can control third-party cookies through your browser settings or our cookie preferences.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Browser Settings">
    Control cookies through your browser. You can manage cookies using your browser settings:

    **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
    **Firefox**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
    **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
    **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

    <Callout type="info">
      Browser settings affect all websites, not just RIXL.
    </Callout>
  </Card>

  <Card title="Cookie Consent Banner">
    Manage preferences on our site. When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner allows you to:

    - Accept all cookies
    - Reject non-essential cookies
    - Customize your cookie preferences
    - Learn more about our cookie practices

    <Callout type="info">
      You can update your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link in the website footer.
    </Callout>
  </Card>

  <Card title="Opt-Out Options">
    Specific cookie opt-out tools. To opt out of specific cookies, use the following options:

    **Google Analytics**: [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

    **Advertising Cookies**: [Your Online Choices](https://www.youronlinechoices.com/) or [Network Advertising
    Initiative](https://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/)

    <Callout type="warn">
      Opting out may affect the functionality and personalization of our services.
    </Callout>
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may limit the functionality of websites and services. For example, you may need to log in repeatedly.

- Your preferences and settings may not be saved

- Some features may not work as expected

- We may not be able to provide personalized experiences

Essential cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of the website and cannot be disabled.

## Other Tracking Technologies

We also use additional tracking technologies alongside cookies:

### Web Beacons

These are small, transparent images that help us understand user behavior and email engagement.

### Local Storage

Local storage maintains your preferences and improves performance.

### SDKs and APIs

Our software development kits collect usage data to enhance functionality and performance.

### Log Files

Log files record technical details about your interactions with our services.

## Data Protection and Privacy

### Data Security

We implement security measures to protect cookie data and ensure it is used only for its intended purposes.

### Data Retention

Cookies are retained for different periods depending on their purpose:

- Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser

- Functional cookies: Typically 1–2 years

- Analytics cookies: Usually 2 years

- Marketing cookies: Varies by provider, typically 1–2 years

### International Transfers

Some cookie data may be processed by service providers in other countries. We ensure safeguards are in place for these transfers.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect cookie data from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a child has provided us with cookie data, please contact us.
