## Current Plan Summary

Your billing dashboard shows:
- **Active Plan:** Current subscription tier and monthly cost
- **Billing Cycle:** Next billing date and payment method
- **Usage Metrics:** Real-time tracking of storage and API usage
- **Recent Invoices:** Quick access to billing history
