Welcome to RIXL! Our platform helps creators and businesses process, optimize, and deliver media quickly and easily, no matter the size of your project.

## What is RIXL?

RIXL makes it easy to manage videos and images for any type of app. Whether you’re working on a social platform, an e-learning site, a portfolio, or a mobile app, RIXL takes care of the hard parts of media processing and delivery.
