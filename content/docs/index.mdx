## What RIXL Does

### Media Processing
Instantly transcodes videos to device-optimized formats, compresses images for best performance without quality loss, and tailors delivery to any bandwidth or screen size in real time.

### Content Organization
Offers customizable projects for asset management, enables real-time team collaboration, structures media into searchable feeds, and boosts search accuracy with detailed metadata tagging.

### Global Delivery
Leverages a global CDN for immediate media access, powers smooth adaptive streaming for continuous playback, and transforms content on demand to meet user criteria instantly.

### Developer Integration
Offer a comprehensive API, deliver official SDKs for major languages (coming soon), and monitor events to simplify automation and workflow integration.

## Platform Architecture

RIXL offers two main ways to use the platform, and they work well together:

**Dashboard** – Visually upload, organize, and manage media. Use drag-and-drop uploads, project organization, team collaboration, and analytics to streamline your workflow.

**API Platform** – Integrate applications with REST endpoints. Perform full CRUD operations, process media in batches, and monitor activities in real time.

## Supported Formats

| Media Type | Formats | Max File Size | Output Formats | Notes |
|------------|---------|---------------|----------------|-------|
| **Video** | MP4, MOV, AVI, WMV, WebM, MKV | up to 10GB | MP4, WebM, HLS, DASH | Automatic transcoding to web-optimized formats |
| **Images** | JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, AVIF, HEIC, TIFF | up to 100MB | JPEG, PNG, WebP, AVIF | Smart compression with quality preservation |
| **Audio** | MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG | up to 1GB | MP3, AAC, OGG | Audio extraction from video also supported |
