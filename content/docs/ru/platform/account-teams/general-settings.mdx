Access your general settings by navigating to **Account Settings → General Settings** from your profile menu.

## Personal Information

Keep your details up to date so teammates and organizations can identify you easily.

### About You

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Display Name:** Your name shown to other organization members
    - Click the edit icon next to your name to modify it
    - Appears in team lists, comments, and shared projects
  </Step>

  <Step>
    **Username:** Your unique identifier (e.g., `ayaaakinleye`)
    - Click the edit icon to change your username
    - Used in URLs and for team identification
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Account Email

Your email address is your login and is used for account notifications.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Enter your new email address in the **Account Email** section.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click **Update** or **Save Changes**
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Check your inbox for a verification email
    - Status shows "Pending" until verified
    - Click **Resend** if you don't receive it
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Your email is visible to organization admins and may be used for invitations or account recovery.
</Callout>

## Account Preferences

Set your preferred language, region, and notifications.

### Language & Region

- **Language**: Interface language (currently English)
- **Timezone**: Used for activity timestamps and billing cycles
- **Date Format**: How dates appear across the interface

### Notification Preferences

Control how you receive updates about your account and projects:

- **Email Notifications**: Project billing and security updates
- **In-App Notifications**: Real-time alerts  while logged in
- **Team Communications**: Mentions, invitations, and collaboration updates

<Callout type="tip">
  To customize notifications for specific organizations, go to the [Membership](/platform/account-teams/membership)
  section.
</Callout>

## Account Management

### Account Status

Check your current account status and any required actions:

- **Verification Status**: Email verification and profile completion
- **Account Type**: Personal or organization-managed
- **Account Limitations**: Temporary restrictions or pending actions

### Export Account Data

Download a copy of your account information and activity:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Account Management → Request Data Export**
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Choose what data to include:
    - Profile information and settings
    - Project and media activity logs
    - Organization memberships and roles
  </Step>

  <Step>
    You'll receive a download link via email within 24 hours
  </Step>
</Steps>
