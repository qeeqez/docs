Access your general settings by navigating to **Account Settings → General Settings** from your profile menu.

## Personal Information

Keep your account details up to date so teammates and organizations can identify you easily.

### About You

<Steps>
    <Step>
        **Display Name:** Your name visible to other organization members
        - Click the edit icon next to your name to modify it
        - Appears in team lists, comments, and shared projects
    </Step>

    <Step>
        **Username:** Your unique identifier (e.g., `ayaaakinleye`)
        - Click the edit icon to change your username
        - Used in URLs and for team member identification
    </Step>
</Steps>

### Account Email

Your email address is your primary login and is used for account notifications.

<Steps>
    <Step>
        In the **Account Email** section, enter your new email address
    </Step>

    <Step>
        Click **Update** or **Save Changes**
    </Step>

    <Step>
        Check your inbox for a verification email
        - You'll see a "Pending" status until verified
        - Click "Resend" if you don't receive the verification code
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Your email is also visible to organization admins and may be used for invitations or account.
</Callout>

## Account Preferences

### Language & Region

Configure your preferred language and regional settings:

- **Language**: Interface language (currently English)
- **Timezone**: Used for activity timestamps and billing cycles
- **Date Format**: How dates appear throughout the interface

### Notification Preferences

Control how you receive updates about your account and projects:

- **Email Notifications**: Updates on projects, billing, and security 
- **In-App Notifications**: Real-time alerts  while logged in
- **Team Communications**: Mentions, invitations, and collaboration updates

<Callout type="tip">
To customize notifications for specific organizations, go to the [Membership](/docs/platform/account/membership) section.
</Callout>

## Account Management

### Account Status

View your current account status and any required actions:

- **Verification Status**: Email verification and profile completion
- **Account Type**: Personal or organization-managed
- **Account Limitations**: Any temporary restrictions or pending actions

### Export Account Data

Download a copy of your account information and activity:

<Steps>
    <Step>
        Go to **Account Management → Request Data Export**
    </Step>

    <Step>
        Choose what data to include:
        - Profile information and settings
        - Project and media activity logs
        - Organization memberships and roles
    </Step>

    <Step>
        You'll receive download link via email within 24 hours
    </Step>
</Steps>
