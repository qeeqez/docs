Organizations help you group projects together and manage your team in one place. Here's how to create one.

### Creating Your Organization

<Steps>
    <Step>
        Go to **Organization Settings** in your dashboard.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Click **Create New Organization**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        **Fill out the form:**
        - **Organization Name:** Pick a name for your team or company.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Click **Create Organization** to finish.
    </Step>
</Steps>

After creating your organization, you'll be the owner. You can then invite people, create projects, and set up billing.

### Team Size Planning

<Tabs defaultValue="small" items={["small", "medium", "large"]}>
    <Tab value="small">
        **Small Teams (3-10 people)**

        - Owner handles most administrative tasks
        - Members work on specific projects
        - Simple role structure is sufficient
    </Tab>
    <Tab value="medium">
        **Medium Teams (10-50 people)**

        - Designate Admins for different departments
        - Project-based access control becomes important
        - Consider delegating billing oversight
    </Tab>
    <Tab value="large">
        **Large Organizations (50+ people)**

        - Multiple Admin roles for different areas
        - Department-based project organization
        - Detailed usage and cost tracking
    </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Post-Creation Setup

### Essential Steps

<Steps>
    <Step>
        **Add Your Team**
        Invite the people you work with and give them the right roles.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        **Create Your First Projects**
        Set up projects and decide who can access them.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        **Set Up Billing**
        Add your payment method and choose a plan.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        **Make Rules**
        Decide how your team will create and approve content.
    </Step>
</Steps>

### Organization Management Tasks

- **Control Access:** Decide who can access what projects and features
- **Handle Billing:** Manage billing and subscriptions for your organization
- **Manage Projects:** Create projects and control who has access to them
- **Set Up Security:** Configure security rules and authentication requirements
- **Monitor Usage:** Keep track of how much your organization is using

## Best Practices

<Tabs defaultValue="Naming" items={["Naming", "Security"]}>
    <Tab value="Naming">
        **Choosing a Name**

        - Use a clear name that describes your business
        - Avoid special characters or excessive abbreviations
        - Consider how it will appear in web addresses
        - Choose something that will scale with growth
    </Tab>
    <Tab value="Security">
        **Security Setup**

        - Require two-factor authentication
        - Set reasonable session timeouts
        - Be careful about role assignments
        - Check permissions regularly
    </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type='warning'>
    Organization settings affect everyone and all projects. Think carefully about security settings before you apply them.
</Callout>
