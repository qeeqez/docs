## Payment Methods

### Current Payment Method

Your active payment method is displayed in the billing dashboard, showing:
- **Card Type:** Visa, Mastercard, etc.
- **Last 4 Digits:** For security, only the last 4 digits are shown
- **Expiration Date:** Month and year when the card expires

### Supported Payment Options

RIXL accepts the following payment methods:
- **Credit Cards:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **Debit Cards:** Cards with major network logos
- **International Cards:** Multi-currency support for global users

<Callout type="info">
    Payment information is securely processed and encrypted. Card details are never stored on RIXL servers.
</Callout>

### Payment Issues & Troubleshooting

If a payment fails, you'll receive immediate notification and have a **7-day grace period** to resolve the issue.

Common solutions:
1. **Verify Card Details:** Check card number, expiration, and CVV
2. **Confirm Billing Address:** Must match your card's registered address
3. **Check Bank Restrictions:** Some banks block international transactions
4. **Sufficient Funds:** Ensure adequate account balance
5. **Try Alternative Card:** Use a different payment method

<Callout type="warning">
    Service may be suspended after the 7-day grace period if payment issues aren't resolved.
</Callout>

## Invoices

### Your Invoice History

Find your invoices in the Recent Invoices section:
- Invoice number (INV-001, INV-002, etc.)
- Date created
- Amount charged
- Payment status
- Download button

### Download Invoices

<Steps>
    <Step>Find the invoice you want</Step>
    <Step>Click **Download** in the Actions column</Step>
    <Step>PDF downloads to your device</Step>
</Steps>

**Each invoice includes:**
- Billing period dates
- Monthly subscription fee
- Any overage charges
- Taxes
- Payment method used

### Email Notifications

You'll get emails for:
- New invoices
- Successful payments
- Failed payments
- Payment retries
