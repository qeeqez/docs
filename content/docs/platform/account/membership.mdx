## Current Organization Memberships

View all organizations you belong to and your role within each.

### Membership Overview

Your [membership dashboard](/platform/account/membership) displays:
- **Organization Name**: The name of each organization you belong
- **Your Role**: Your specific role (Owner, Admin, Member)
- **Join Date**: When you became a member
- **Status**: Active, pending, or any restrictions

### Project-Level Permissions

Within each [project](/platform/dashboard/projects/overview), you may have additional specific permissions:
- **Media Upload**: Create and upload new content
- **Media Management**: Edit and delete existing content
- **Feed Management**: Create and configure content feeds
- **Analytics Access**: View project performance data
- **Settings Access**: Modify project configuration

## Troubleshooting Membership Issues

### Common Issues

**Can't access a project:**
1. Verify your organization membership status
2. Check project-specific permissions
3. Contact your organization admin

**Missing permissions:**
1. Review your current role
2. Check if permissions were recently changed
3. Request access from organization owners/admins

**Organization not appearing:**
1. Check pending invitations
2. Verify email address match
3. Contact the organization administrator
