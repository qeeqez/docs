## Current Plan

Your current subscription details are displayed at the top of the billing dashboard, showing:

- **Plan Type:** Your active subscription tier
- **Monthly Cost:** Billing amount and cycle
- **Next Billing Date:** When your next payment is due
- **Payment Method:** Last 4 digits of your payment card

### Changing Your Plan

<Steps>
    <Step>
        Navigate to **Project Settings → Billing**.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Click **Change Plan** to view available options.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Select your desired plan from the modal window.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Review pricing and confirm the change.
    </Step>
</Steps>
