The [Billing section](https://api.rixl.com/settings/billing) allows you to manage your subscription plans, payment methods, usage tracking, and invoice history.

<Callout type="info">
Navigate to **[Organization Settings → Billing](https://api.rixl.com/settings/billing)** to access all billing features.
</Callout>

## Plan Management

Your dashboard displays:

<Tabs items={['Plan Details', 'Usage Status', 'Billing Cycle']}>
<Tab value="Plan Details">
- Plan name, pricing, and feature limits
- Support level and active add-ons
</Tab>
<Tab value="Usage Status">
- Storage and API usage vs. limits
- Team member count and trends
</Tab>
<Tab value="Billing Cycle">
- Current period, next billing date
- Payment method and upcoming charges
</Tab>
</Tabs>

### Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
<Step>Go to **Organization Settings → Billing** → **Upgrade Plan**</Step>
<Step>Select target plan and billing cycle (monthly/annual)</Step>
<Step>Review pro-rated billing and confirm upgrade</Step>
</Steps>

### Downgrading Your Plan

<Callout type="warning">
Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Please ensure your usage stays within the new plan limits.
</Callout>

## Payment Methods

### Supported Options
- Credit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Debit cards with major networks
- Multi-currency international support

### Managing Payment Methods

<Tabs items={['Add Method', 'Update Cards', 'Set Default']}>
<Tab value="Add Method">
<Steps>
<Step>Go to **Billing → Payment Methods** → **Add Payment Method**</Step>
<Step>Enter card details and complete billing address</Step>
<Step>Save and verify the payment method</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>
<Tab value="Update Cards">
You may update card information, billing addresses, and expiration dates for your existing payment methods at any time.
</Tab>
<Tab value="Set Default">
Set your preferred card for automatic billing and subscription renewals.
</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Usage Overages

<Callout type="warning">
If you exceed your plan limits, additional fees will apply to ensure uninterrupted service.

<Tab value="API Overages">

## Payment Issues

<Callout type="info">
A 7-day grace period is provided for failed payments, including multiple retry attempts and notification alerts.
</Callout>

<Steps>
<Step>Verify payment method and billing address accuracy</Step>
<Step>Confirm sufficient account balance and no bank restrictions</Step>
<Step>Update payment information if needed</Step>
<Step>Contact billing support for persistent issues</Step>
</Steps>
