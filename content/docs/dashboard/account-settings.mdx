Personal Settings enables you to control account preferences, security, and membership details. This section covers features for your individual RIXL account, not for organization settings.

<Callout type="info">
Go to **[Personal Settings > Account](https://api.rixl.com/settings/account/general)** to find all personal account features and preferences.
</Callout>

## Account Management

### Profile Settings

<Cards>
  <Card title="Personal Information" description="Manage your name, username, and basic profile details" />
  <Card title="Account Settings" description="Configure two-step verification and connected accounts" />
  <Card title="Membership Management" description="View and manage team memberships and invitations" />

#### Basic Profile Information

<Tabs items={['Name & Username', 'Profile Details']}>
<Tab value="Name & Username">
**Name**
- Display name shown across the platform and visible to team members
- Can be updated at any time

**Username**
- Automatically generated from your email address (uses part before @)
- Serves as unique identifier and cannot be manually changed
- Updates automatically when email changes
</Tab>
<Tab value="Profile Details">
**Email Address**
- Primary login credential and contact method
- Must be verified to maintain account access

**Profile Customization**
- Upload custom profile picture
- Set timezone and language preferences
</Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="tip">
Your username is generated from your email address and will update automatically if your email changes.
</Callout>

## Security Settings

### Two-Step Verification (2FA)

<Steps>
<Step>
Navigate to [Personal Settings → Account → Security](https://api.rixl.com/settings/account/security) and click **Set up Two-Step Verification**
</Step>
<Step>
Scan the QR code using Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the 6-digit code from your app to verify setup
</Step>
</Steps>

### Connected Accounts

<Cards>
  <Card title="Google Account" description="Sign in with Google for seamless authentication and enhanced security" />
  <Card title="Apple Account" description="Use Apple ID for privacy-focused authentication with Touch ID or Face ID" />
  <Card title="Telegram Account" description="Connect with Telegram for notifications and content sharing" />
</Cards>

<Tabs items={['Adding Connections', 'Managing Connections']}>
<Tab value="Adding Connections">
<Steps>
<Step>Go to **[Personal Settings → Account → Security](https://api.rixl.com/settings/account/security)** → **Connected Accounts**</Step>
<Step>Click **Connect** next to your preferred service</Step>
<Step>Follow the authentication flow and authorize RIXL access</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>
<Tab value="Managing Connections">
**Account Management:**
- View all connected accounts and their status
- Click **Disconnect** to remove connections
- Ensure alternative login methods are available before disconnecting
</Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="info">
Connected accounts offer enhanced security and convenience through single sign-on capabilities.
</Callout>

### Invitations Management

<Tabs items={['Sent Invitations', 'Received Invitations']}>
<Tab value="Sent Invitations">
- View all [invitations sent](https://api.rixl.com/settings/account/invites) to potential team members
- Track status: sent, viewed, accepted, or declined
- Resend or cancel pending invitations
</Tab>
<Tab value="Received Invitations">
- See all [pending invitations](https://api.rixl.com/settings/account/invites) from other organizations
- Accept or decline invitations
- Manage invitation preferences and notifications
</Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="tip">
Review team memberships and invitations regularly to keep operations efficient and access controlled.
</Callout>

## Account Actions & Security

### Session Management

<Cards>
  <Card title="Single Session Logout" description="Log out of current device only while keeping other devices signed in" />
  <Card title="Complete Account Logout" description="Signs you out on all devices at once. Use this if your account is compromised." />

### Security Best Practices

<Callout type="warning">
Follow these practices to reduce the risk of unauthorized access and protect your content and collaborations.
</Callout>

**Essential Security Measures:**
- Always enable two-step verification with an authenticator app
- Use strong, unique passwords and update regularly
- Review active sessions and connected accounts monthly
- Monitor membership changes and invitation activity

## Troubleshooting

<Tabs items={['Login Issues', 'Two-Factor Problems', 'Connection Issues']}>
<Tab value="Login Issues">
**Common Login Problems:**

<Steps>
<Step>
**Verify Credentials**

Check email address and password accuracy, ensure caps lock is off
</Step>
<Step>
**Clear Browser Data**

Clear browser cache and cookies, ensure stable internet connection
</Step>
<Step>
**Password Recovery**

Use "Forgot Password" link on login page and check email for reset instructions
</Step>
<Step>
**Contact Support**

If issues persist, contact support with account verification details
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>
<Tab value="Two-Factor Problems">
**2FA Troubleshooting Steps:**

<Steps>
<Step>
**Check App Sync**

Ensure authenticator app time is synchronized with your device
</Step>
<Step>
**Verify Setup**

Confirm QR code scanning was successful and app is updated
</Step>
<Step>
**Reset 2FA**

Re-set up 2FA if access is restored or contact support for assistance
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>
<Tab value="Connection Issues">
**Connected Account Problems:**

<Steps>
<Step>
**Verify Third-Party Account**

Check if connected service credentials are correct and account is active
</Step>
<Step>
**Re-authorize Connection**

Disconnect and reconnect problematic accounts
</Step>
<Step>
**Clear Service Data**

Clear browser cookies for connected services and update account settings
</Step>
<Step>
**Contact Support**

Reach out for persistent connection issues with account details
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>
</Tabs>
