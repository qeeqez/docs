## Overview

Your Rixl account is your gateway to managing media assets, creating projects, and collaborating with
team members. This guide will walk you through creating your account and managing your account settings.

## Create Your Rixl Account

To get started with Rixl, you'll need to create an account:

<Steps>
    <Step>
        ### Visit the Sign Up Page

        Navigate to the Rixl registration page at `https://rixl.com/signup`
    </Step>

    <Step>
        ### Enter Your Details

        Provide your email address and create a strong password (minimum 8 characters with complexity requirements)
    </Step>

    <Step>
        ### Verify Your Email

        Check your inbox for a verification email from Rixl and click the verification code or link to confirm your email address
    </Step>

    <Step>
        ### Complete Your Profile

        Once verified, log in to complete your profile setup with your name and preferences
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
    Your account name defaults to your email address initially. You can customize this later in your account settings.
</Callout>

## Account Security

Protect your account with these security best practices:

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters
- Must include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Must include numbers or special characters
- Cannot reuse previous passwords

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Enhance your account security with time-based one-time passwords:

<Steps>
    <Step>
        ### Enable 2FA

        Navigate to **Security Settings** â†’ **Two-Factor Authentication**
    </Step>

    <Step>
        ### Scan QR Code

        Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.) to scan the QR code
    </Step>

    <Step>
        ### Save Backup Codes

        Download or copy your backup codes and store them securely
    </Step>

    <Step>
        ### Verify Setup

        Enter a code from your authenticator app to complete the setup
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn">
    Save your backup codes in a secure location. Without them, account recovery may require additional verification steps.
</Callout>

## Profile Management

Customize your account information and preferences:

### Personal Information

- **Full Name**: Your display name across the platform
- **Username**: Your unique identifier for mentions and references
- **Profile Picture**: Optional avatar image
- **Language Preference**: Interface language setting


## Account Types

Rixl offers different account types based on your needs:

### Personal Account

- Single user access
- Basic project creation
- Standard support

### Team Account

- Multiple user collaboration
- Advanced project management
- Priority support
- Organization settings

<Callout type="info">
    You can upgrade from a Personal to Team account at any time through your account settings.
</Callout>

## Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to maintain account security and organization:

### Email Address

- Use a professional email address for business accounts
- Consider using an email alias for team accounts (e.g., rixl@yourcompany.com)
- Keep your email address updated for important notifications

### Account Organization

- Use descriptive account names for easy identification
- Regularly review connected applications and API keys
- Remove unused integrations and services

### Security Maintenance

- Regularly update your password
- Review active sessions and logged-in devices
- Enable 2FA for enhanced security
- Monitor account activity for unusual behavior

## Account Recovery

If you lose access to your account:

### Password Reset

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Follow the instructions in the reset email
4. Create a new strong password

### 2FA Recovery

1. Use your backup codes to regain access
2. Contact support if you've lost both your 2FA device and backup codes
3. Verify your identity through account recovery questions
