        ## General Settings

        Manage your personal information and account details.

        ### About You

        Update your display name and username as they appear across RIXL and your organizations.

        <Steps>
            <Step>
                **Name:** Your display name visible to other organization members
                - Click the edit icon next to your name to modify it
            </Step>

            <Step>
                **Username:** Your unique identifier (e.g., `ayaaakinleye`)
                - Click the edit icon to change your username
            </Step>
        </Steps>

        ### Account Email

        Your email address is used for logging in and account notifications.

        <Steps>
            <Step>
                In the **Account Email** section, enter your new email address
            </Step>

            <Step>
                Click **Update** or **Save Changes**
            </Step>

            <Step>
                Check your inbox for a verification email
                - You'll see a "Pending" status until verified
                - Click "Resend" if you don't receive the verification code
            </Step>
        </Steps>

        <Callout type="info">
            The address shown (e.g., `youremail@gmail.com`) is used to identify your RIXL Account to you and others.
        </Callout>

    </Tab>

    <Tab value="Security">

        ## Security Settings

        Keep your account secure with strong authentication and connected accounts.

        ### Connected Accounts

        Link third-party services for single sign-on (SSO) access to RIXL.

        **Currently Supported:**
        - **Google:** Quick and secure login with your Google account
        - **Apple:** Privacy-focused authentication with Apple ID
        - **Telegram:** Connect for additional security features

        <Steps>
            <Step>
                **To Connect an Account:**
                - Click **Connect** next to the desired service
                - Follow the authorization prompts from the provider
            </Step>

            <Step>
                **To Disconnect an Account:**
                - Click **Disconnect** next to the connected service
                - Confirm the action when prompted
            </Step>
        </Steps>

        <Callout type="warn" title="Before Disconnecting">
            If you use a connected account as your only sign-in method, set a password for your RIXL account **before** disconnecting it to avoid being locked out.
        </Callout>

        ### Two-Step Verification

        Add an extra layer of security with authenticator app-based 2FA.

        <Steps>
            <Step>
                Click **+ Setup authenticator** in the 2-Step Verification section
            </Step>

            <Step>
                Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator recommended)
            </Step>

            <Step>
                Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app to complete setup
            </Step>
        </Steps>

        The description notes: "Get verification codes from an authenticator app. It works even if your phone is offline."

    </Tab>

    <Tab value="Memberships">

        ## Organization Memberships

        View and manage your role in different organizations.

        ### Current Memberships

        This section displays:
        - **Organization:** The name of each organization you belong to
        - **Role:** Your role within the organization (e.g., "owner")
        - **Actions:** Available management options

        <Steps>
            <Step>
                Review your current organization memberships in the table
            </Step>

            <Step>
                Click **Manage** next to any organization to access organization-specific settings
            </Step>

            <Step>
                Use the **Actions** column to perform role-specific tasks
            </Step>
        </Steps>

        ### Organization Management

        For organizations where you have owner or admin privileges, you can:
        - Manage team members
        - Adjust organization settings
        - Handle billing and subscription details
        - Control access permissions

    </Tab>

    <Tab value="Invites">

        ## Pending Invitations

        Manage invitations to join organizations or projects.

        ### Invitation Status

        <Steps>
            <Step>
                **No Invites Found:** When you have no pending invitations, this section will display "No invites found"
            </Step>

            <Step>
                **Refresh:** Click the **Refresh** button to check for new invitations
            </Step>

            <Step>
                **Accept/Decline:** When invitations are present, you'll see options to accept or decline each invitation
            </Step>
        </Steps>

        ### Managing Invitations

        When you receive invitations, you can:
        - View the organization or project name
        - See who sent the invitation
        - Check the proposed role or permissions
        - Accept or decline the invitation
        - View invitation expiration dates (if applicable)

        <Callout type="info">
            Invitations may have expiration dates. Be sure to respond promptly to avoid missing opportunities to join organizations or projects.
        </Callout>

    </Tab>
