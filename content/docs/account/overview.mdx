### What You Can Do

<Steps>
	<Step>
    **Profile Settings**

    Update your name and email.
    </Step>

	<Step>
    **Security**

    Change your password, Setup two-factor authentication, and check active login sessions.
    </Step>

	<Step>
    **API Access**

    Create and manage API keys for your apps.
    </Step>

	<Step>
    **Organizations**

    See your teams, manage your role, and accept team invitations.
    </Step>

	<Step>
    **Billing**

    View billing settings for organizations where you have access.
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
To find these settings, click your profile picture in the top-right corner and select **Account Settings**.
</Callout>
